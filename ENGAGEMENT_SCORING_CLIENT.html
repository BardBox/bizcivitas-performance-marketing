Section 02

How It Works

The system silently tracks visitor behavior and builds a score. No forms, no surveys — just real behavior data.

The Journey in 4 Steps

1
Visitor arrives on your website
The moment someone lands on your performance marketing page, tracking begins automatically. Every scroll, every click, every second spent — it all counts.
2
Behavior is scored in real-time
Each action earns points. Browsing for 30 seconds? +3 points. Checking pricing? +15 points. Starting to fill a form? +8 points. The score builds silently.
3
Lead is categorized into a pipeline stage
Based on their total score, the visitor is automatically placed into New, Cold, Warm, or Hot. Your team instantly knows who to prioritize.
4
Payment converts the lead
When a lead completes payment through Razorpay, they become "Converted" — regardless of their score. Payment is the ultimate proof of intent.

Fully Automatic: No manual tagging, no subjective judgment. The system assigns scores based on actual behavior, ensuring consistent and unbiased lead evaluation.

What's Being Tracked?

The system watches 11 different types of user behavior, each worth a different number of points based on how strong a buying signal it represents:

Browsing Signals
Page visits, return visits, time spent on page, scroll depth, multiple pages viewed
Intent Signals
CTA clicks, pricing page visit, form started, form submitted, file downloads

Score Thresholds are Adjustable

The boundaries between stages (11, 26, 51) are not fixed. You can change them from the admin panel at any time. For example, if you want leads to reach "Warm" faster, lower the warm threshold from 26 to 20.

Section 08

Score Decay

Keeping your pipeline fresh by reducing scores for inactive leads.

The Problem It Solves

Imagine a lead who visited your site 3 months ago, scored 55 points (Hot), but never came back. Are they still "Hot"? Probably not. Score decay ensures your pipeline stays accurate over time.

How It Works

30
Days of Inactivity
After 30 days without any activity, the decay triggers (configurable)
-10
Points Deducted
10 points are removed per decay cycle (configurable)

Decay Examples

Converted leads are never decayed. Once someone has paid, they remain "Converted" permanently — no matter how long they've been inactive.

Section 11

Data & Privacy

How visitor data is handled responsibly.

What Data Is Collected

Data TypeWhen CollectedPurpose
Behavioral actions (clicks, scrolls, time) During website visit Calculate engagement score
Session identifier First page visit Group actions in one session
Return visit flag On return visits Identify returning visitors
Personal info (name, email, phone) Only when user submits the inquiry form Lead management & follow-up

Important: No personal information is collected until the visitor voluntarily submits the inquiry form. Before that, only anonymous behavioral data is tracked in the browser's session storage.

Data Storage

Before Form Submit
Events stored only in the visitor's browser (sessionStorage). Nothing is sent to the server. Data disappears when the tab closes.
After Form Submit
Events are synced to the server and permanently stored with the lead record. Full activity history is available in admin.

Access Control

  • Only admin users can access lead data and scoring configuration
  • Admin panel is protected by JWT authentication with httpOnly cookies
  • Public tracking endpoints only accept anonymous event data
  • Scoring configuration requires admin authentication to modify

Data Retention

Lead data and activity logs are stored indefinitely for business intelligence. Score decay reduces scores but does not delete activity logs — you can always see the full history of a lead's engagement even after decay reduces their current score.

Section 12

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the lead scoring system.

Q: Can I change the scoring values?
Yes! Go to /admin/scoring in the admin panel. You can adjust every event's point value, toggle events on/off, and change pipeline thresholds. No developer needed.
Q: What happens if I change a score after leads already have points?
Existing lead scores are NOT retroactively changed. The new scores only apply to future events. If a lead already earned 15 points for "Pricing Visit" and you change it to 25, their existing 15 points stay.
Q: Can a lead go from Hot back to Warm?
Only through score decay. If a Hot lead is inactive for 30+ days, their score decreases and they may drop back to Warm. Active leads never lose points.
Q: Can a Converted lead ever lose their status?
No, never. Once payment is verified, "Converted" is permanent. Score decay does not affect converted leads.
Q: Does the tracking slow down my website?
No. Tracking runs silently in the background using lightweight browser events (scroll listeners, timers). No visible impact on page load or user experience.
Q: What if a lead visits from different devices?
Pre-inquiry events are session-based (per browser tab). Once the lead submits the inquiry form, all future tracking is linked to their email address across devices.
Q: Can I add my own custom scoring events?
Yes! From the admin panel, you can create unlimited custom events with any name and point value. You can also edit and delete custom events anytime.
Q: How often should I check the admin panel?
We recommend checking Hot leads at least twice daily (morning and evening). Warm leads should be reviewed daily. Cold and New leads can be checked weekly.
Q: Can I reset everything to default values?
Yes. The scoring configuration page has a "Reset to Defaults" button that restores all 11 system events to their original scores and resets thresholds and decay settings.
Q: Is the data shared with anyone?
No. All lead data stays in your BizCivitas database. It is only accessible to authenticated admin users through the admin panel.
